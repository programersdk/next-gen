Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our pricing and plans

Is there a free trial available?

Yes, we offer a 30-day free trial for both Starter and Professional plans. You'll have full access to all features during the trial period with no obligation to continue.

Can I change plans later?

Absolutely! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. When upgrading, you'll be prorated for the remainder of your billing cycle. Downgrades will take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and for our international customers, we also support regional payment methods like JazzCash and EasyPaisa.

Do you offer discounts for multiple schools?

Yes, we offer volume discounts for school districts or organizations with multiple institutions. Please contact our sales team for custom pricing based on your specific needs.

Is there a setup fee?

No, there are no setup fees for any of our plans. We believe in transparent pricing with no hidden costs.

What support is included?

All plans include email support with a 24-hour response time. Professional and Enterprise plans include priority support with faster response times. Enterprise customers also receive a dedicated account manager.
